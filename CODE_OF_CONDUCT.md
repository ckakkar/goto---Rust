# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We are committed to a harassment-free, inclusive, and respectful community for
everyone, regardless of background or identity.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent.
- Focus feedback on code and behavior, not people.
- Help keep discussions welcoming and professional.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks
- Trolling, insulting, or derogatory language
- Publishing private information without consent
- Any conduct that a reasonable person would consider inappropriate

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing this policy.
They may remove, edit, or reject content and participation that violates this
Code of Conduct.
